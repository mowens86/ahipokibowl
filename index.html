A Bowl of Vacay

In Every Bite

If a tropical vacation, good weather, sandy beaches, and delicious seafood sound amazing, take a bite of our poke, you'll get a taste of Hawaii in every bite.

Ocean of rice

Cover the bottom with floral-scented jasmine rice, whole grains or traditional white rice. Going green this month? Start with a bed of lettuce.

Modern seafood

Choose your catch of the day! From protein-packed yellowfin tuna to flavorful salmon, seafood will have your belly feeling happy and satisfied.

Mountain of toppings

Dive into a medley of crunchy, spicy, salty and fruity toppings. We’re talking jalapeños to wake up your taste buds, sweet (but low-cal) pineapples and so much more. The combinations are endless!

Tasty sauce

Don’t be afraid to add a lil’ something extra to your poke. Drizzle on salty soy sauce or rich, creamy aioli.
